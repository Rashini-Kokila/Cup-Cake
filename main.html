COVID 19

Thank you all who have been patient with us while we ensured everyone at Toronto Cupcake and their families were safe. We have now re-opened for business and with the following protocals in place we are able to supply you with all the essential cupcakes you need!!

  • 24 hour online ordering.
  • Order anytime before 5PM and we will 'No contact' deliver for you the following day. We are not able to process same day orders for the forseeable future.
  • We have a minimum order of a dozen. As the situation changes we will bring back 1/2 doz ordering.
  • At this time pick up is not available. We are providing contactless delivery for all orders.

Once again thank you for choosing Toronto Cupcake. Please stay safe and take care of your love ones.

About michelle


Toronto Cupcake was created by Michelle Harrison so she could pursure her love of baking. A lifelong baker, inspired by her mother, Michelle opened Toronto Cupcake in August 2010 as one of Canada's first gourmet cupcakeries.
